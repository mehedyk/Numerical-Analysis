How do I type my equation?

The parser reads standard math notation. A few examples to get you oriented:

You wantType this
Powersx^3, x^2
Multiplication4*x (or just 4x)
Square rootsqrt(x)
Trig functionssin(x), cos(x), tan(x)
Exponential, eˣexp(x)
Natural loglog(x) or ln(x)
Base-10 loglog10(x)
Constantspi, e
Absolute valueabs(x)
Heads up: in this parser, log(x) means the natural log, same as ln(x) — not base-10. Use log10(x) if that's what you mean.

Full example: sin(x) - x/2 or exp(-x) - x work exactly as written.
